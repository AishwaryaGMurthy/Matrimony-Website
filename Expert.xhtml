Tips for selecting your partner!!!

  1. Once decided to marry, be clear about your preferences regarding status, age, qualification, job, Income etc. preferably by discussing with all parties concerned.

  2. Photos play a vital role. Send only recent ones. Have adequate copies.

  3. Verify the genuiness of the data in the profile tactfully and carefully. Visit the parties in person. Collect references.

  4. Meeting of Bride and Groom - Choose the appropriate stage and Venue. Allow them to interact freely.

  5. Do not give over importance for qualification and income and never get carried away by the charms of external appearance. Inner qualities or the character is in fact more important.

  6. Nothing wrong in accepting persons with minor disability. It must be taken as a matter of pride and not shame.It is more important to judge whether the character of the person suits your mind.

  7. Never get dejected over delay in getting suitable alliances.
